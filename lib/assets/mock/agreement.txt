
### Privacy Policy

KYN app is a digital application platform to connect people around the nearby localities in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. This app makes it easy for people to get aware of the nearby happenings and also post about the same in image, text, and video formats. This platform makes it more engaging for registered users by viewing such posts and interacting with them.

This Privacy Policy will help you understand what data we collect, why, and what we do with it. By downloading this app, you understand and accept the contents of this privacy policy and its subsequent versions. We appreciate & acknowledge the informed consent granted by you while downloading the KYN App, using its features, and/or submitting your information within the App.

**Collections**

To provide a tailor-made and personalized experience, the information you share may be processed and analyzed by third-party services who are engaged by us with due confidentiality obligations and solely for the specific purpose of such services. This will help the App give you the best possible experience by showing according to your interests. For example, based on the location data collected, you may get a push notification when posts are published from that location in the future. Similarly, based on your previous interactions within the App you may receive push notifications based on your interests in the future.

**Use of Information**

We limit the collection and use of non-public information to the minimum we believe is necessary to show more recent, relevant, and appropriate information to you.

**Permissions**

The KYN App requests several permissions in order to serve you better. All these permissions are contextual and cause-specific. You are also free to grant or deny many of these permissions. Below is a list of permissions requested along with the purpose they serve:

**Identity and Accounts**

Permission to use your identity and use relevant information from your device to help you use your Google Account, and Facebook to log in to the App. You may choose to not grant this permission and enter your identity details independently.

**Contacts**

Permission to access your contacts is requested to help you connect with your friends to form a community.

**Camera and File Storage**

Permission to access your camera and/or photos/media/files is requested to help you use features like Notice, Telecast, Shorts, and other such initiatives where posting a photo or video may be required to participate. These permissions are also required to enable you to post photos or videos anywhere on the App.

**Read SMS**

Permission to access your SMS to read OTP sent over SMS to authenticate the login. This permission may also apply to Merchandising, Donations, or Token management sections of the App in the future. Users can choose not to grant this permission.

**Microphone**

Permission to access your microphone may be requested only to enable you to converse in video and audio facilities in the future.

**Phone**

Permission to access your phone is requested only to enable you to place phone calls to access the Contacts section of the App from which you can make calls directly if needed.

**Wi-Fi/Network Connection/Network Access**

Permission to access Wi-Fi, Network Connection, and Network Access is taken only to allow the App to connect to the internet to provide its functionality.

**Draw over other apps**

Permission to draw over other apps is taken only to allow for a modern and contextual user interface that can be showcased outside the App.
The permissions asked for are used only for a specific feature or user action. For example, permission to use photos is used only when you want to post a photo.

**Use**

The following information collected by the App is used only for the purposes mentioned below:
- User Photo is used to display in the user profile section and also alongside the contents posted by the user.
- Gender is used to address you correctly in notifications and other communications
- Name is used to display in the user profile & alongside your content, personalized notifications, and communications.
- Email ID is used to send important communications if any.
- Location details are used to serve you content relevant to your geography and also to send notifications in your language, in the future.
- IP Address is used as a security feature to log malicious activity if any.
- Phone Number is used to send OTP for KYN App login and also to send SMS regarding any important information from KYN 2.0 App team if any in the future.
- Device information is used to create accounts, perform contextual actions, personalized services and provide personalized services.
- Unique Application numbers, cookies, and similar technologies are used to identify your device uniquely for contextual services and push notifications.

**Transparency and Choice**

You are free to grant or deny the permissions listed above and utilize the (when asked for) App with a subset of the features you choose to avail at any given time.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. When we change the policy in a material way, a notice will be posted on our website along with the updated Privacy Policy.

**User Safety and Security**

We value all personal information provided by you and we shall ensure the safety and security of the information you provide in the KYN App. Your data shall not be sold or rented to anyone and will only be utilized for the functionality of the App and related features, services, etc.

No form of tolerance is accepted in case of objectionable content and abusive users on the KYN app. Such contents will be removed by the compliance team that works 24*7.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or data usage, please write to us in the Help & Feedback section of the KYN App.
